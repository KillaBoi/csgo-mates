Track suspicious players

To track a supicious player first you need to search for him or for a game you are playing together in the home tab. Then simply click on the icon at the end of his line in the table of players, he will now appear in the Tracked player tab.

If you want to stop tracking him just click on the icon again

Get notified when suspected players get banned

You will be notified when a player you are suspecting is getting VAC banned or overwatch banned by a change in the suspect icon on the menu.

This time when you'll access the suspect section, the players caught cheating will appear on red.

Suspicions are shown on players profile

If you suspect a player it will show on his profile so that other players playing against him can see it. It means that if you choose to suspect someone you are committing on the fact that this player is probably cheating, so be careful on who you suspect!
